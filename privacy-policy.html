Privacy Policy – Radio RTEF

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Overview

Radio Tele Evangelique Florida ("RTEF", "we", "us") operates the RTEF mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

Data We Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number unless you voluntarily contact us.

We may collect anonymous, non-personal data including:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App usage statistics (via third-party analytics)
  • Crash reports to improve stability

How We Use Data

  • To provide and maintain the radio and TV streaming service
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To respond to support requests

Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services that collect data used to identify you. These include:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase (crash reporting)

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these services.

Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Internet access – required for streaming audio and video content
  • Wake lock – to keep the screen on during playback

Data Retention

We do not store personal data on our servers. Any anonymous analytics data is retained only as long as necessary to improve the service.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

Your Rights

  • Request information about data we hold
  • Request deletion of any personal data
  • Opt out of analytics by adjusting your device settings

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted within the app and on this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: rtefmedia@gmail.com
